Hello, my name is
Alessandro Melone.

I am currently a Research Associate at Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence (MIRMI) of the Technical University of Munich based at Munich, Germany.

During my time at MIRMI, I've been involved in diverse research projects, supporting methodology, experimentation, and scholarly writing. I followed closely a European project (I.AM.) of which MIRMI was a partner (ended at March 2024). I conducted my Master's thesis at the German Aerospace Center (DLR), where I studied Differential Geometry to implement a sequential convex optimizer to solve trajectory optimization problems on Lie groups manifolds. I studied Robotics and Automation at the University of Federico II of Naples, where I got a background in control theory, signal processing, and optimization. I specialized on Robotics during my master (as reference, the book Robotics: Modelling, Planning and Control). I studied Computer Vision and participated in a course project working on an Image Segmentation problem using the Keras framework.
